Crystal Collector


How do you even play this crazy game??


  1. A random number will magically be generated!! a.k.a. "Number To Get"
  2. The long lost crystals of Avalon have been found after centuries in someones closet, so use them wisely...
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your power points.
  4. You win the game by matching your power points to the "Number To Get".
  5. You lose the game if your power points go above the "Number To Get".
  6. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
